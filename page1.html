My Education

Right now, I’m studying computer science at the University of Windsor. Despite already being in my third year, I’m still not really sure what kind of work I want to do once I graduate, to be honest… I mean, computer science is used in every single industry at this point, which means there are so many options to choose from! The problem is: how could I possibly know which one(s) I like without trying them first? I’ve been taking a variety of classes to try lots of new things, and it’s helping me a bit...

This semester, I chose to take Artificial Intelligence Concepts to explore my interest in AI, and Intro to the Internet to try website-making. I’m finding both classes to be interesting, but I’m really enjoying Intro to the Internet because I get to be creative with my assignments, which makes them super fun!
